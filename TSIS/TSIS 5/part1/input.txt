A Song of Ice and Fire is a series of epic fantasy novels 
by the American novelist and screenwriter 
  George R. R. Martin. 
He began the first volume of the series, 
    A Game of Thrones, in 1991, and it was published in 1996. 
Martin, who initially envisioned the series as a trilogy,
has published five out of a planned seven volumes. 
The fifth and most recent volume of the series, A Dance with Dragons, 
  was published in 2011    and took Martin six years to write. 
He is currently writing the sixth novel,The Winds of Winter.
